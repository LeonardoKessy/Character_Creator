What is Character Creator: D&D Edition?

Character Creator: D&D Edition is a web application designed to help you build fully-fledged characters based on the rules and spirit of Dungeons & Dragons. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer preparing for your next campaign or a newcomer exploring the realms for the first time, this tool will guide you every step of the way.

Build Your Character Step by Step

The creation process is broken into intuitive steps: name, background, race, class, attributes, and starting equipment. As you make choices, your character sheet updates in real time â€” so you can see your adventurer take shape immediately.

Choose Your Race, Class, and Destiny

Select from classic D&D races like Humans, Elves, Dwarves, Halflings, and more. Pair them with iconic classes such as Fighter, Wizard, Rogue, or Cleric. The app ensures your choices follow D&D 5e rules, including valid race- combinations, bonuses, and restrictions.

Save and Manage Your Heroes

Once your character is complete, you can save it for future sessions, edit it as your story evolves, or duplicate it to explore new builds. The generated sheets follow D&D's standard layout, ready to bring to your table.
